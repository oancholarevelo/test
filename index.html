Effective Study Techniques

The Pomodoro Technique

Work in focused 25-minute intervals, separated by short breaks. This method improves focus and prevents burnout. Use a timer to stay on track.

Learn More

The Feynman Technique

To truly understand a concept, try to explain it in simple terms as if you were teaching it to someone else. Identify gaps in your knowledge.

Learn More

Active Recall

Actively retrieve information from your memory instead of passively rereading it. This strengthens neural pathways for long-term retention.
